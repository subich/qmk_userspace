# Planck keymap

My personal layout for the planck 40% ortholinear keyboard

### Notes
- Enter functions as shift when held
- Left space activates NUMPAD layer when held
- 1PASS key activates 1Password mini when pressed

## Qwerty
```
 ,-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------.
 | Esc  |   Q  |   W  |   E  |   R  |   T  |   Y  |   U  |   I  |   O  |   P  | Bksp |
 |------+------+------+------+------+-------------+------+------+------+------+------|
 | Tab  |   A  |   S  |   D  |   F  |   G  |   H  |   J  |   K  |   L  |   ;  |  "   |
 |------+------+------+------+------+------|------+------+------+------+------+------|
 | Shift|   Z  |   X  |   C  |   V  |   B  |   N  |   M  |   ,  |   .  |   /  |Enter |
 |------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------+------|
 |Numpad| Ctrl | Alt  | GUI  |Lower |Space |Space |Raise | Left | Down |  Up  |Right |
 `-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------'
```
